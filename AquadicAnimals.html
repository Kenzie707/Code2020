Aquatic animals have been treated unfairly and used for humans, to be
happy. People train these animals to show off what they’ve done to them, how
these people hurt these animals. Aquatic animals are taken away from their
homes in fishing nets and put into tanks, so we humans are satisfied and get
a show.
If people keep on buying and seeing these animals go through tourture, then
aquatic animals will continue to be used for our enjoyment. This will lead to
more and more animals being in pain to just live up to our expectations. This
has to be stopped. These animals risk their lives for our entertainment and
satisfaction.
People of today need to stop paying to see these animals in glass prison cells.
They are suffering from something they can’t control and we need to give these
the freedom they deserve. People just shouldn’t stand around and do nothing.
They need to help these animals become free.
